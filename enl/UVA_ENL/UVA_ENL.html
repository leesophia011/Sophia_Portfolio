Based in Charlottesville, UVA Health


embodies the leadership and ingenuity of Thomas Jefferson, who established the nation’s 10th medical school in 1825, which has since grown into a nationally renowned academic medical center. UVA Health encompasses three inpatient facilities — a 600-bed acute care hospital, a transitional care hospital and a children’s hospital.
